FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about staking VTRS with VNRG Node.

What is VNRG Node's commission rate?

VNRG Node charges a fixed 20% commission on all staking rewards. The remaining 80% is distributed directly to delegators proportionally to their stake every epoch — with no surprises or hidden fees.

How often are VTRS staking rewards distributed?

Staking rewards on the Vitreus network are distributed per epoch. You can claim or re-stake your VNRG rewards at any time through the Vitreus Collaborative Marketplace.

Is staking VTRS with VNRG Node safe?

When you delegate VTRS to VNRG Node, your tokens are staked directly on-chain. You remain in full control of your delegation and can unstake at any time through the Vitreus marketplace. VNRG Node does not take custody of your tokens. As with all staking, rewards are not guaranteed and staking involves risk.

What is the minimum VTRS required to stake with VNRG Node?

The minimum staking amount is set by the Vitreus Collaborative Marketplace. Visit marketplace.vtrs.io and check the VNRG Node listing for current minimum and capacity details.

What happens if the VNRG Node validator goes offline?

VNRG Node is backed by UPS battery backup power and a dedicated leased internet line to ensure maximum uptime. Our infrastructure is designed to keep the node online through power interruptions and network disruptions. Current uptime is 99%+.

What is the VNRG token and how is it different from VTRS?

VTRS is the primary staking and governance token of the Vitreus blockchain. VNRG (gVolts) is the utility rewards token earned through staking VTRS. When you delegate VTRS to VNRG Node, you earn VNRG rewards, which can be used within the Vitreus ecosystem, traded, or converted back to VTRS.
