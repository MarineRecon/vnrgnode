Methodology

How the Calculator Works

The calculator uses a simplified annualised reward model based on VTRS staking mechanics. VNRG Node takes a 20% commission; delegators receive the remaining 80%.

// Gross annual reward
gross_annual = vtrs_staked × (apy / 100)

// Delegator share after 20% commission
delegator_annual = gross_annual × 0.80

// Monthly estimate
monthly = delegator_annual / 12

// Per epoch (~daily estimate, Vitreus network)
per_epoch ≈ delegator_annual / 365

// Total over chosen duration
total = monthly × months
